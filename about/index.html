Born & raised in Kansas City, Missouri, I've always been interested in technology, but I never knew what specifically and I didn't want to stay at home for college. So, in 2015, after graduating from Lincoln Prep high school, I knew that it was time to venture out and find out what I want to do with my life besides play video games & watch Breaking Bad. This led me to attending the Illinois Institute of Technology in the Fall. Initially a Computer Science major, I did not feel that I as engaged as I should have been. I wanted something more interactive, more hands-on. So, I decided to switch to Information Technology & Management w/ a minor in Psychology, and I've never looked back since. I am studying now to become a professional Web Designer and/or Hardware specialist. I think I've finally found out what I wanted to do. YAY!
