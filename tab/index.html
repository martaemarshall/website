Why did I make this page

As an African-American, you don't learn a lot about your people in the field of technology in school. It is usually people like Thomas Jefferson, Steve Jobs, Bill Gates, people who are not like you. So, I created this page to give the black community some unfimiliar faces that will encourage them to go into technology and change the world.
